模運算基本特性:
$$
(a + b) \% p = (a \% p + b \% p) \% p \\
(a - b) \% p = (a \% p - b \% p) \% p \\ 
(a * b) \% p = (a \% p * b \% p) \% p \\
(a ^ b) \% p = ((a \% p) ^ b ) \% p
$$
模運算的结合律：
$$
((a + b) \% p + c) \% p= (a + (b + c) \% p) \% p \\
((a * b) \% p * c) \% p = (a * (b * c) \% p ) \% p
$$
交換律：
$$
(a + b) \% p = (b+a) \% p \\
(a * b) \% p = (b * a) \% p
$$
分配率：
$$
((a +b) \% p * c) \% p = ((a * c) \% p + (b * c) \% p) \% p
$$
